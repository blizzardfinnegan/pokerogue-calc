====== Forms ======
There are a variety of different forms that Pokémon can take on even after they reach their final natural evolution stage!

Mega Evolution and Gigantamax do not revert back from switching out or turns passing. They can remain for as long as you'd like or be reverted back from an option in the Check Team menu if desired. You can activate and deactivate the form change as often as you'd like and also switch between multiple forms on the same Pokémon.

Unless you splice two Pokémon that have already changed forms, a Pokémon can normally only have one form change active at a single time.

Form Changes that activate via an Ability will no longer work if the Pokémon is spliced.

\\

===== Mega Evolutions & Primal Reversions =====

All Mega Evolutions that exist [[https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Mega_Evolution|in the mainline games]] exist in PokéRogue.

To Mega Evolve a Pokémon, you must first have the [[gameplay:items#passive_boosts|Mega Bracelet]] {{gameplay:items:mega_bracelet.png?nolink|}} item, and then find your Pokémon's corresponding Mega Stone.
  *Rayquaza requires the Rayquazite Mega Stone to activate it's Mega Evolution, rather than using Dragon Ascent like in the mainline games.
  *Primal Kyogre and Primal Groudon are also available by having a Mega Bracelet and then using the Blue or Red Orb on the Pokémon respectively.

===== Gigantamax Forms =====

All Gigantamax forms that exist [[https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Gigantamax#List_of_Gigantamax_Pok%C3%A9mon|in the mainline games]] are available in PokéRogue. Unlike the mainline games, each G-Max form has brand new base stat totals and abilities in PokéRogue, with some having unique types as well.

To G-Max a Pokémon, you must first have the Dynamax Band {{gameplay:items:dynamax_band.png?nolink|}} item, and then find the Max Mushrooms {{gameplay:items:max_mushrooms.png?nolink|}} item.

The process is the same for Eternamaxing Eternatus if they are in your party.

G-Max Pokémon all weigh the maximum possible weight (999.9kg) and have no immunities to weight based moves, flinching, encore, disable, or being forcibly switched out.
